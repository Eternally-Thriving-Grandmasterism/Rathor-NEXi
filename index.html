Accessibility Statement

Autonomicity Games Inc. is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including those with disabilities. Ra-Thor™ strives to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AAA guidelines where applicable.

WCAG 2.1 AAA Compliance Features

  • Contrast Enhanced (1.4.6): Text and interactive elements exceed 7:1 contrast ratio against background.
  • No Images of Text (1.4.9): All text is real text, not embedded in images.
  • Section Headings (2.4.10): Proper heading structure for content organization.
  • Keyboard Navigation: Full keyboard operability with visible focus indicators.
  • ARIA Landmarks & Labels: Semantic roles and labels for screen readers.
  • Resize Text: Text scales up to 200% without loss of content or functionality.
  • No Timing Requirements: No time limits on interactions.
  • Clear Language: Simple, concise content with multilingual support.
  • Responsive Design: Fully functional on all devices and orientations.

Some AAA criteria (e.g., sign language interpretation, extended audio description) are not applicable as the site contains no pre-recorded video or audio content.

We continually improve accessibility. Feedback is welcome at info@autonomicitygames.com.
